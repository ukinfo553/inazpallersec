Customer Success Stories

Finance

The BFSI is one of the highest risk sectors when it comes to cybersecurity. Banks and financial institutions deal with highly sensitive personal and financial information that must be protected at all times.

A leading bank with more than 8000 branches across the world came to us with the objective of strengthening their cybersecurity resilience and making their systems hack-proof.

Challenge

The company needed to train its workforce on cybersecurity awareness as well as on new secure technologies so they could quickly migrate to a secure system while minimizing any disruptions to existing operations.

Solution

We tailored a comprehensive training program which constituted cybersecurity awareness training for all employees as well as incident response training to their tech teams in a blended learning model (combination of self-paced and LIVE Instructor-led sessions).

Retail

Organizations in retail have to deal with huge volumes of data for customer insights and analysis. This data is crucial and requires protection as well as data mining to produce actionable business insights.

Our client is a well-known retail chain in Asia with hundreds of stores and thousands of employees across the world. They sought to bolster their cybersecurity posture and data privacy mechanisms proactively.

Challenge

The company faced the daunting task of training its diverse workforce, spanning corporate offices, distribution centers, and retail outlets, on cybersecurity best practices to reach and engage employees at all levels, from executives to frontline associates.

Solution

We customized our program to the retail environment by including interactive modules on topics like point-of-sale security, data privacy, and social engineering awareness along with modules on recognizing and responding to potential threats in real-world scenarios.

Technology

In the fast-paced technology sector, where innovation and rapid software development are crucial, integrating security into the development lifecycle is paramount. A leading software company recognized the need to adopt a DevSecOps approach to ensure the delivery of secure and compliant applications while maintaining agility and speed.

Challenge

The software company faced the challenge of seamlessly integrating security practices into their existing DevOps processes without slowing down the development and release cycles, while ensuring compliance with industry standards and regulations.

Solution

Through our comprehensive DevSecOps program, we enabled the software company to shift security left, integrate security into their development processes, and deliver secure and compliant applications while maintaining their agility and speed to market.

Healthcare

In the healthcare industry, where patient privacy and data integrity are critical, cybersecurity breaches can have devastating consequences. A leading healthcare provider sought to strengthen its governance, risk, and compliance (GRC) posture to protect sensitive medical records, ensure regulatory compliance, and safeguard patient trust.

Challenge

The healthcare provider faced the challenge of aligning its operations with the latest industry regulations and best practices for GRC. They needed to establish robust policies, procedures, and controls to manage risks effectively and maintain compliance.

Solution

Our GRC consultancy team conducted a comprehensive assessment of the healthcare provider's existing governance, risk, and compliance frameworks. We assisted in establishing a robust governance structure & implemented a risk management methodology tailored to their operations.
