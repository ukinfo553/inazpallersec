European Privacy Training - CIPP/E Online Training & Certification Course
 
Certified Information Privacy Professional/ Europe (CIPP/E) certification is developed by the International Association of Privacy Professionals (IAPP). This certification is intended for professionals to enhance their knowledge and establish them as an expert in Data Protection. The training covers the concepts of Data Privacy and Data Protection, European Data Protection laws and regulations, 99 Articles of GDPR Regulation, and more. The CIPP/E certification recognises and demonstrates your knowledge in various parts of Information Privacy domain by analyzing your skills.
