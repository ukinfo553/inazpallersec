State Agency for Comprehensive Health and Integrated Services (SACHIS) in collaboration with its technical partner ACCESS Health, Department of Medical Education and Roche India Healthcare Institute (RIHI) cordially invites you to the Capacity Building Program to strengthen capacities for Cancer Care in Uttar Pradesh.

The capacity building program aims to increase standard of care for cancer care through increased awareness, compliance to Standard Treatment Guidelines and collaborative learning among the PM-JAY hospital network in the state. This programme will include five major cancer types- breast, oral (head and neck), lung, hematology, and liver.
We are delighted to begin the training on July 15, 2022 under the Chairmanship of Smt Sangeeta Singh (IAS) Chief Executive Officer, SACHIS as per the following session details.
